Featuring.

White Beach
A world-famous stretch of powdery white sand and vibrant sunset views in Boracay.
Big Lagoon
A breathtaking limestone-framed lagoon in El Nido with crystal-clear, emerald waters.
Kayangan Lake
Known as the cleanest lake in the Philippines, offering turquoise waters and scenic limestone cliffs.
Chocolate Hills
A surreal landscape of over a thousand grass-covered hills that turn chocolate-brown in summer.
Tubbataha Reef
A UNESCO-protected marine sanctuary famed as one of the world’s best diving destinations.
Vigan
A beautifully preserved Spanish colonial town with cobblestone streets and heritage houses.
Intramuros
The historic walled city of Manila that showcases centuries of Spanish influence.
Magellan's Cross
A symbolic Christian landmark in Cebu planted by the expedition of Ferdinand Magellan.
Cloud 9 Surfing Spot
Siargao’s legendary surf break known for its powerful barrels and global surf competitions.
Rizal Park
A national historical park in Manila dedicated to Philippine hero Dr. Jose Rizal.
Adobo
A savory Filipino dish of meat simmered in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic.
Lechon
A fiesta staple of whole roasted pig known for its crispy skin and juicy meat.
Sinulog
A grand and colorful Cebu festival honoring the Santo Niño through lively street dances.
Ati Atihan
A tribal-themed festival in Aklan celebrated with rhythmic drumbeats, face paint, and street parades.
Apo Whang Od
The oldest traditional Kalinga tattoo artist, revered as the last mambabatok of her generation.
